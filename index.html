Overview SOBHA NEOPOLIS Panathur, Off Marathahalli-ORR, Bengaluru

Sobha Neopolis stands as a magnificent testament to luxury living, nestled in the serene locales of Panathur Road, Bangalore. This grand residential endeavor by Sobha Limited spans a lush 25-acre expanse, designed to embody the essence of a stately urban sanctuary. The project presents a selection of opulent apartments available as 1, 3, and 4 BHK units, each crafted to offer the epitome of comfort merged with the finesse of fine artistry. These luxury residences, ranging from expansive 1600 sq ft to lavish 2400 sq ft options, are a blend of Grecian elegance and contemporary design, ensuring an upscale living experience with ample space, natural light, and ventilation for absolute privacy and well-being. Rising up to 18 floors and featuring over a thousand units, including special 3 BHK + study configurations, Sobha Neopolis is not just an address but a Mediterranean retreat promising a lifetime of happiness, health, and opulence. With plush amenities tailored for all ages set in a picturesque Greek-themed township, it offers a unique blend of global fittings and elite designs for an expansive lifestyle. Discover a world where luxury meets comfort at Sobha Neopolis, your gateway to a perpetually energized life.
